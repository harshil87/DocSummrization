The motivation behind developing a Legal Document Summarizer using machine learning and NLP stems from the pressing need to address the formidable challenges faced by legal professionals in managing the everincreasing volume of complex legal documents. This innovative solution aims to alleviate the time-consuming and labour-intensive nature of document analysis, enhancing the efficiency and productivity of legal practitioners. By automating the summarization process, it enables legal experts to make informed decisions more quickly, reduces the risk of oversight, and ultimately enhances the quality and costeffectiveness of legal services, driving progress in the legal field.
